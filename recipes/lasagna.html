Lasagna

Description

To make a classic lasagna, start by preheating your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a large pan, heat olive oil and sauté diced onions and minced garlic until soft and fragrant. Add ground beef, cooking until browned, then stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15–20 minutes. While the sauce cooks, boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, an egg, half of the mozzarella, Parmesan, and fresh parsley or basil. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place a layer of noodles on top, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture, then more meat sauce. Repeat the layers, finishing with a final layer of meat sauce and the remaining mozzarella on top. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Dried Basil
  • Dried Oregano
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Egg
  • Fresh Parsley or Basil
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until soft and fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it up as it cooks.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Let the meat sauce simmer for 15–20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Boil the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions, drain, and set aside.
  8. In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, half of the mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and fresh parsley or basil.
  9. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
  10. Add a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  11. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture on top of the noodles.
  12. Add another layer of meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
  13. Repeat the layers of noodles, ricotta mixture, and meat sauce.
  14. Finish with a final layer of meat sauce and top with the remaining mozzarella cheese.
  15. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  16. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  17. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before serving.
